I am a Senior DevOps Consultant at [Eficode](https://www.eficode.com) and Professor in Software Systems at [Aalto University](https://www.aalto.fi/en) in Finland.

### Consulting

In my consulting job, I support customers in their digital transformation through effective software development lifecycle and platform engineering. I assess customers' toolchains and DevOps practices, typically ahead of a migration. I am especially engaged with the strategic and architectural aspects of migrating to GitHub Enterprise. I also deliver GitHub training on actions, administration, git, and AI-assisted development with Copilot. I am a [Certified Backstage Associate](https://www.credly.com/badges/e294c0fa-e0cd-4913-a320-694be6f67277) and hold multiple GitHub certifications ([Actions](https://www.credly.com/badges/b13a63aa-98a6-4fc1-be4e-f8fe63817c0b), [Administration](https://www.credly.com/badges/57ca8c19-0519-4deb-aeec-76bea03fb994), [Advanced Security](https://www.credly.com/badges/0611f0fc-b6b9-4cc2-b322-929718f07e74), [Copilot](https://www.credly.com/badges/59611ac4-f125-4b08-acd1-83b96a04ae3e), [Foundations](https://learn.microsoft.com/api/credentials/share/en-us/mdifra/6B974A742BCE545?sharingId=7E267388E8A3A825)) as well as technical partner delivery credentials ([Azure DevOps to GitHub Enterprise Migrations](https://www.credly.com/badges/76c78ccb-8ab7-4c77-a1b3-265d0a381095/), [GitHub Advanced Security](https://www.credly.com/badges/3eff84cc-3c9c-4444-9a35-e1f5d5006caf/), [GitHub Copilot: Core Skills & Application](https://www.credly.com/badges/1c4e0369-2b12-4bff-a14e-d4ff78dd8c72), [GitHub Migrations](https://www.credly.com/badges/d1e6e4b7-4acc-4c5f-abe3-b362bc2fee5c)).

### Academia

In my academic role, I carry out research on network systems – broadly defined. My goal is to understand and develop software technologies, particularly in the context of cloud, mobile, and embedded systems. I am interested in both theory and practice, and I seek to bridge the two of them. I am [currently working](https://scholar.google.fi/citations?hl=en&user=xuJ3KAYAAAAJ&view_op=list_works&sortby=pubdate) on microservice security, efficient machine learning, and the Internet of Things. I also teach courses on [cloud computing](https://sisu.aalto.fi/student/courseunit/aalto-OPINKOHD-1142276610-20210801/brochure) and [DevOps](https://fitech.io/en/studies/introduction-to-devops-aalto/). <!-- I secured more than two million euros funding from the Research Council of Finland and over $750,000 from the US National Science Foundation. -->


### Software

I publish the outcome of my research as open-source software, including:

- [Kubesonde](https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3611643.3613899) ([source](https://github.com/kubesonde/kubesonde)), a tool to assess microservice connectivity in Kubernetes;
- [FLoRa: A framework for LoRa simulations with OMNeT++](https://flora.aalto.fi/) ([source](https://github.com/florasim/flora)), a component that implements the LoRa long-range wireless communication protocol for the OMNeT++ simulator;
- [Distance-Dependent Barcodes for Context-Aware Mobile Applications](https://dawcode.aalto.fi/) ([source](https://github.com/aaltodawcode/dawcode-android)), mobile-friendly barcodes that encode different information based on the distance they are scanned from;
- [Automated Assessment of Android Exercises with Cloud-native Technologies](https://dl.acm.org/doi/abs/10.1145/3341525.3387430) ([source](https://github.com/orgs/aolecloudscape/repositories)), a container-based system to automatically grade Android assignments using mobile app testing principles.
